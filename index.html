Is your County in Trouble?

Wisconsin's obesity rate has more than doubled over the last 20 years. It is well known that obesity can lead to other diseases like diabetes, but do we know what it is that causes obesity? Factors such as access to workout facilities and availability of fresh foods can play a role. Additionally, economic status can either allow or limit the ability to pay more for higher quality foods and fitness resources.
